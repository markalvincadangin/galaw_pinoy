Description

Virtual blockers move horizontally across the screen. The player steps left or right to avoid contact.

Rules

  1. The player stays within the camera frame.
  2. Blockers move across the screen.
  3. The player sidesteps left or right to avoid blockers.
  4. Getting hit ends the round.
  5. Difficulty increases after each successful dodge.
