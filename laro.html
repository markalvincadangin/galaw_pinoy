Most Played Laro ng Lahi in the Philippines

These traditional games have been played for generations and continue to shape Filipino childhood and culture.

Luksong Baka

A jumping game that develops leg strength, balance, and courage.

Luksong Tinik

Promotes flexibility, coordination, and timing.

Patintero

Builds agility, teamwork, and strategy.

Piko

Improves balance, coordination, and focus.

Tumbang Preso

Develops accuracy, speed, and agility.

Agawan Base

Enhances endurance, teamwork, and strategic thinking.

Langit-Lupa

Improves reaction time and cardiovascular endurance.
